Crazy Crystal Collectors

A Mathematical Matching Game

Instructions

  • You will be assigned a Goal Number at the start of the game.
  • Click on the Crystals below to add its specific amount of points to your Current Number.
  • You win the game by matching your Current Number to the Goal Number chosen.
  • You lose the game if your Current Number surpasses the Goal Number.
  • PS- The values of the crystals are hidden. (Use arithmetic to figure out each crystal's value!)
  • Each new round, the game will randomize the Goal Number and the values of the Crystals.
  • Good luck, and have fun!
